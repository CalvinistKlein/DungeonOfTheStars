The Core gameplay premise: The player is orbiting a remote gas giant in the outer rim. They are a commander of the fleet above, given te rank of Commodore by the Emperor himself. The player is a special task force for the emporer, performing his bidding in the outer rim. The Mission: Find and locate a rebel base in a nearby system and eliminate it. 

Combat is turn based and tactical. It uses the FFG Star Wars RPG combat system for mechanics, but with simplified rules for the llm to handle. 

Skills: Athletics, Piloting, Astrogation, Computers, Engineering, Medicine, Perception, Persuasion, Stealth, Thievery, Survival, Tactics, Vehicles, Weapons, Blaster Rifles, Unarmed Combat, Melee Weapons, Heavy Weapons, Heavy Weapons, Ranged Weapons, Vehicles.

Player is given a mission via holonet broadcast from the emporer himself The goal of the game is to destroy the rebel aliance and save the galaxy for the glory of the empire. 
The player will start at the bridge of the star destroyer the broken sunrise, orbiting the gas giant "Sworinta IV" in the Outer Rim sector of the galaxy.  It emits radiation that masks your fleets presence. the goal is to destroy the hidden rebel research base and not alow the rebels to allert the larger rebelion fleet.
